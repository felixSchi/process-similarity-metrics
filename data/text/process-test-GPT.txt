Frist, receive customer requirements with at least 5 properties. Then, order the guide bar based on required length. Then, order the chain based on required width. Then, order the motor or electric drive components. Then, order the housing components. Then, order the safety and control components. After that, receive all parts. And inspect all parts manually. Before you assemble the first chainsaw. You send first status update to customer. After that, send second status update to customer. Then, ship first the chainsaw to customer. After that, collect the customer feedback. Lastly, produce the remaining chainsaws.
